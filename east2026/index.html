2026 East Coast Regional Gathering

On May 5-7, 2026, we invite those ministering in Chinese heritage churches – particularly in the eastern states, to gather for fellowship, mutual encouragement, and collaboration.

The schedule reflects the desire for our gathering to focus not only on content, but more so on connections with others ministering in Chinese heritage churches, particularly on the eastern United States.

Register

  • $250.00 until Saturday, February 28, 2026 (then $300.00)
  • Includes lodging and six meals (Tuesday dinner through Thursday lunch)
  • Registration closes on Sunday, April 12, 2026 or until we are at capacity
  • There is no on-site registration.
  • Questions? Contact info@chineseheritagechurch.com.
