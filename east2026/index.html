2026 East Coast Gathering

On May 5-7, 2026, we invite those ministering in Chinese heritage churches – particularly in the eastern states, to gather for fellowship, mutual encouragement, and collaboration.

The schedule reflects the desire for our gathering to focus not only on content, but more so on connections with others ministering in Chinese heritage churches, particularly in the eastern United States.
